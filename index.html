Timeline of Adele's Life

  1. 1988 - Born in Tottenham, London.
  2. 2006 - Graduates from Brit School for Performing Arts & Technology in Croydon.
  3. 2007 - Releases breakthrough song "Hometown Glory" written when she was 16.
  4. 2008 - Becomes first recipient of Brit Awards Critic's Choice.Releases first abulm ,19, and embarks on the "An Evening With Adele" world tour.
  5. 2009 - 19 abulm certified gold by RIAA. Recieves Grammy Awards for Best New Artist and Best Female Pop Vocal Performance

  7. 2015 - Releases third album, 25, and it becomes her second album to be certified diamond in the US.
  8. 2017 - At the 59th Annual Grammy Awards, Adele won all five of her nomination , bringing her number of awards to fifteen.
  9. 2020 - Adele announced that her fourth album release has been delayed due to COVID-19 pandemic.
  10. 2021 - Releases fourth album, 30.Recieves Brit Award for British Album of the Year 2022.
